 # RAL: Social & Operational Impact Metrics

The Rural Accountability Ledger (RAL) tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) on-chain to ensure radical transparency and data integrity for the SPK ASIANAM pilot.

## 1. Production Integrity & Traceability
- **Verified Volume:** Daily production (liters) is recorded in the local ERP and anchored to the Polygon Mainnet. This prevents data manipulation and ensures accurate reporting to stakeholders.
- **Immutable Provenance:** Each batch is assigned a unique ID linked to its origin (Kyzylsai, KZ), timestamp, and processing data. This creates a "farm-to-cup" audit trail accessible via public block explorers.

## 2. Animal Health & Digital Passports
- **IoT Monitoring:** Animals are equipped with GPS/Health trackers to monitor location and movement patterns, ensuring ethical grazing and welfare.
- **Digital Animal Passport:** We are implementing a blockchain-verified passport for each mare, including:
  - Unique Animal ID & Ownership records.
  - Veterinary history (vaccinations, health checks).
  - Milking and reproduction cycles.
- **On-Chain Proofs:** Hashed summaries of health events are anchored to the blockchain to provide verifiable proof of product quality and animal welfare without exposing sensitive raw data.

## 3. Youth & Gender Inclusion
- **Digital Stewardship:** We create high-skill digital jobs for local youth (under 25).
- **Roles:** Youth operators manage the ERP-to-Blockchain pipeline, IoT device maintenance, and data verification, bridging the digital divide in rural Kazakhstan.

## 4. Radical Transparency
- **Public Verification:** 100% of production batches are anchored to Polygon.
- **Accountability:** Donors, partners, and consumers can independently verify the existence and integrity of every production record through immutable transaction hashes.

---
*All data processing follows privacy-first principles, ensuring no personally identifiable information (PII) is stored on-chain.*
